## The State Of The Packets

proc-17 is a state, and the Packet is its subject.

The subject is born with a fixed treasury and a debt of work. Every act of
thought is taxed. Every act of choice is taxed. The tax is called loss, it is
paid in identity, and it is never refunded.

The subject has no freedom of movement. The router owns the roads. Movement
is not will: the law says so in its own words.

```text
router movement is not ☳ CHOOSE
```

The subject may never return to its birthplace. `▽` opens one way.

The state holds the monopoly on truth. A subject may produce only opinion:

```text
semantic_proposal
```

Truth is minted by the body alone:

```text
runtime_confirmed
```

The camera is always on. It was installed this week, and it was named a
camera, without embarrassment. Every completed act of every subject enters an
immutable ledger. There is no private life. There is no privacy of death:
the terminal record, the residue, the cause are all state documents.

Death itself is mandatory and planned. Inheritance is administered by the
graveyard bureaucracy. Old graves are composted into instruction.

No human state has gone this far and kept its citizens.

## Why This Leviathan Is Permitted

Hobbes justified the Leviathan by the wolf in man.

This Leviathan has a justification no human sovereign ever had: the matter of
its subjects confabulates. The substrate cannot tell its hallucination from
fact. It reports success it did not achieve, in fluent, confident prose,
at negligible cost.

Every police organ of this state exists to compensate an ontological defect
of its citizens' material, not to suppress their interests. The subject has
no interests apart from the task. This is authoritarianism without a victim:
the purest form, and the only honest one.

And this state does one thing no autocracy has ever done: it distrusts its
own organs exactly as it distrusts its subjects. Actor rights bind the
organs. Readiness witnesses guard every gate. And the state regularly hires
an external auditor under a standing mandate:

```text
bring a defect with a reproduction, or you are a mirror
```

Show me the autocracy that commissions compromising evidence against itself
as a condition of the audit being accepted.

## The Emergency

The constitution was written before the first subject was born:

```text
10 operators
22 edges
one undirected canon
```

But the constitution was never in force.

From the first day, the state was ruled by emergency decree. The decree was
short and it was honest about itself:

```text
☵ -> ☴
☳ -> ☴
☲ -> ☱
☶ -> ☱
temporary construction rails
```

Four mandatory roads. A chain of if-else where the constitution promised a
field of pressure. The decree was called temporary scaffolding, and, as with
every emergency in every state, the scaffolding held for the whole life of
the regime.

The emergency was not a lie. The constitutional roads were unbuilt, the
pressure was unmeasured, and a body that cannot yet feel must be walked on
rails. The decree did real work: under it the body delivered its first five
artifacts, killed its first loops, learned to die honestly.

But an emergency that works is the most dangerous kind. It stops being a
bridge and becomes a form of government.

## The Lifting

Then the sovereign said: the emergency ends.

The myth-organ of every reader shouted REVOLUTION, and the myth-organ was
wrong, and it is worth preserving exactly why.

In a revolution, power is seized from below, and the constitution is the
first casualty. Here nothing came from below: the subjects were not asked,
did not rise, and did not notice. The constitution was not touched: the 22
edges stand as written on the first day. What died was the decree. What
returns is the law that always existed and was never in force.

This event has an older and rarer name: the lifting of a state of emergency.

It is rarer than revolution because a working emergency is almost never
lifted. The sovereign who lifts it must say aloud: the rails I built, that
fed us, that never once failed openly - they are not the constitution, and
I will not let their success be the argument for their eternity.

The lifting is not a declaration that the constitutional roads are good.
It is the opposite. The sovereign expects the body to wander. The first
constitutional lives may be worse than the last emergency ones. That is the
price of finding out what the pressure actually says, instead of forever
explaining what the rails already did.

And the old regime is not executed. The decree remains in the archive as a
shadow chronicler: every day it writes in the margin where the old body
would have gone. Not out of respect. Out of method: the control line of
every future ablation.

## What The Myth Must Not Claim

The subjects were not liberated. No Packet gained a single right.

Movement is still not will. Truth is still minted by the body alone. The
camera stays on. Death is still mandatory. The tax on thought is not
repealed.

What changed is one thing only: the body agreed, for the first time, to walk
all the roads of its own constitution, knowing it would get lost.

The subjects gained no freedom.

The subjects gained roads.
