## 1. Why This Corpus Exists

The old router is no longer required to move an explicit tree-authority life.
The tree router can derive, commit, execute, manifest, stall, and expose its
evidence while legacy observes without mass.

That does not yet justify making tree authority the default.

The current evidence proves individual mechanisms. It does not yet prove that
the mechanisms survive the important classes of complete Packet life, the
important host boundaries, or the full canonical edge surface together.

The corpus is the final examination before the authority decision. It asks:

```text
Can Packet state govern movement without legacy control?
Does every selected edge have a real witness and readiness proof?
Does every committed edge become executed only after destination work occurs?
Do failures remain typed instead of escaping as harness ambiguity?
Does terminal honesty survive grave and lineage consumers?
Can every record name who reads it and when?
Which legal Tree directions have actually existed rather than merely compiled?
```

## 2. What Counts As Evidence

Promotion evidence must be grown by the body.

```text
real Packet birth
real operator execution through operator_registry
real pressure derivation from Packet state
real route commit through core/packet.lua
real destination execution or an honestly unexecuted boundary
real budget and loss accounting
real terminal/finality when the life ends internally
real grave classification when lineage is claimed
```

Synthetic records remain useful for unit contracts. They do not prove a route,
death, corpse, grave, or lineage behavior for promotion.

Examples of non-promotion evidence:

```text
constructing a grave table by hand
calling edge_stats.record_transition without a living Packet
injecting a DISSOLVE reason directly from the test harness
declaring a CHOOSE case when only one alternative exists
calling max_ticks an internal death
asserting a field exists without proving its named reader consumed it
```

## 3. Two Dimensions Of The Corpus

The corpus has two independent dimensions.

### Behavioral lives

These prove complete phenomena such as success, rejection, mortality, external
failure, host interruption, relation mutation, collapse, and inheritance.

### Canonical edge coverage

The Tree contains 22 canonical edges. Internal edges have two legal directions;
the birth and manifest boundaries have one-life direction laws.

One behavioral life may cover many directions. A green behavioral outcome does
not imply that unvisited edges work. Every executed direction must remain
explicitly counted, and every unvisited direction must remain explicitly
unknown.

Current archaeology reports:

```text
6/22 edges complete in every legal direction
1/22 partial
15/22 without an executed direction
```

The table stage must decide whether step 5 requires every legal direction or a
smaller declared operational surface. It may not silently infer the other
directions from topology alone.

Chaos-level preference:

```text
grow every legal direction that current organs claim they can execute
make every remaining unreachable direction an explicit promotion blocker
or explicitly narrow the promoted authority surface in the step-5 record
```

## 7. Pressure And Calibration Evidence

Agreement with legacy is not the definition of correctness. Legacy is a
historical control and dissent detector, not an oracle.

For each tree derivation the corpus should preserve:

```text
all pressure contributions
whether each contribution varied across relevant cases
source references
calculation status
truth status
candidate score before tie-break
tie-break use
selected readiness
legacy prediction and reason
```

Known pressure risks include relation debt, upper-observation debt, and any
binary source that remains one in every life. A constant can be valid context,
but it cannot be presented as evidence that routing adapted to the task.

Calibration order:

```text
first prove witnesses exist and vary when reality varies
then prove readers consume the intended source
only then discuss weights or thresholds
```

## 9. Session And Lineage Controls

Lineage cases require explicit session boundaries:

```text
fresh session -> empty grave
same session descendant -> selected ancestor graves available
different session -> no accidental inheritance
grave disabled -> control line remains orphaned
```

Generation ids, parent corpse ids, and lineage ids must agree. A descendant
must not inherit global memory merely because files exist on disk.

The blocked-lineage experiment should eventually compare:

```text

line with grave disabled
```

No claim that blocked inheritance teaches can be made until a named reader
changes a measured route, progress state, or cost against that control.
