## 3. Found Gap: The Affordance Witness Is Anticipatory, And C2 Is Circular

For blocking demand, C2 is well-defined: the consumer is blocked NOW; the
fact exists before ☰ runs.

For causal affordance under R1-ENCODE, the chain is:

```text
☰ produces motif -> ☵ consumes motif -> body-owned difference
```

The routing decision the pressure must justify happens BEFORE ☰ runs -
when the motif does not exist yet. So what is the "accepted causal reader
fact" of C2 at that moment? It cannot be the motif (not produced). It
cannot be the consumption difference (not observed). As written, C2 is
either untestable at the pressure moment or quietly circular (pressure
toward ☰ justified by the effect ☰ has not yet enabled).

The witness must be explicitly **anticipatory**: a conjunction of two facts
that DO exist pre-☰:

```text
producer material   uncovered live units inside the consumer's declared
                    domain (coverage layer, already implemented)
consumer need       the consumer's own pending-work witness over that
                    same domain (for ENCODE: unencoded material -
                    encoding_debt's domain - restricted to the units the
                    motif would cover)
```

Affordance pressure toward ☰ = material AND need over one shared domain,
with refs naming both. Either alone is telemetry (the table already says
this for coverage; it must say it for need too, or encoding_debt smuggles
☰ pressure back in).

## 4. Proposed Amendment: Witness Lifecycle, Three Phases

The same witness must change target as the chain advances, or discharge
becomes ambiguous:

```text
phase 1  material + need, no motif        -> pressure toward ☰
         (produce the recognition)
phase 2  motif exists, unconsumed          -> pressure toward the CONSUMER
         (☵ for R1) - NOT toward ☰; more ☰ work is not the bottleneck
phase 3  motif consumed / typed unsupported -> witness discharged or
         transformed into residue
```

Without phase 2, a produced-but-unconsumed motif either keeps pressing ☰
(recurrent producer defect, third edition) or silently stops pressing
anyone (written record with no reader - the signature defect, again,
between ☰ and its consumer). Phase 2 is also where A2's "ENCODE names
consumed relation ids" gets its enforcement moment.

The C-controls then need one added row:

```text

     -> no ☰ contribution; consumer-target contribution present
     -> refs name the unconsumed motif ids
```
